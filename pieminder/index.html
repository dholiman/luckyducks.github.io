What it does

Built for the kitchen,
not the spreadsheet.

1.

Real-time math, no decisions

Adjust pizza count, size, thickness, hydration, and gluten preference. Flour, water, salt, yeast, olive oil, and sugar amounts update instantly (in both grams and ounces).

2.

Instructions simplified

All-in-one screen or guided walkthrough, from chilling water to baking. Each step includes your calculated amounts, so you're free from the cross-reference nightmares.

3.

Timers that live where you look

Mix and rest timers live in your Dynamic Island and on the lock screen. Time-sensitive notifications fire the moment your dough is ready, even with the app closed or after a 3-hour rest.

Pizza night just got a little easier.
