# Privacy Policy — PieMinder Pro

*Last updated: May 2026*

## Overview

PieMinder Pro is a pizza dough calculator. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

## Data We Collect

**Nothing.** PieMinder Pro does not collect any data about you or your device.

## How Your Data Is Stored

Your calculator settings (number of pizzas, pizza size, crust thickness, hydration percentage) are saved **locally on your device** using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

## In-App Purchases

PieMinder Pro offers optional tip jar purchases ($1–$5). All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any transaction details beyond whether a purchase was completed.

## Analytics

None. PieMinder Pro contains no analytics, tracking SDKs, crash reporters, or advertising frameworks.

## Third-Party Services

PieMinder Pro uses no third-party services. The only network activity is StoreKit's connection to the App Store to load tip jar pricing — this is handled entirely by Apple.

## Children's Privacy

PieMinder Pro does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. Given that the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
