Privacy Policy

Last updated · May 2026

Overview

PieMinder is a pizza dough calculator. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data We Collect

Nothing. PieMinder does not collect any data about you or your device.

How Your Data Is Stored

Your calculator settings (number of pizzas, pizza size, crust thickness, hydration percentage) are saved locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

In-App Purchases

PieMinder offers optional tip jar purchases ($1–$5). All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or any transaction details beyond whether a purchase was completed.

Analytics

None. PieMinder contains no analytics, tracking SDKs, crash reporters, or advertising frameworks.

Third-Party Services

PieMinder uses no third-party services. The only network activity is StoreKit's connection to the App Store to load tip jar pricing — this is handled entirely by Apple.

Notifications

If you opt in to timer notifications during the onboarding flow, PieMinder schedules local notifications on your device to remind you when dough timers complete. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on-device by iOS. No notification content is sent through any external server.

Children's Privacy

PieMinder does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. Given that the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
